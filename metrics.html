SRL Benchmarks

SRL, or Semantic Role Labeling, models recover the latent predicate argument structure of a sentence. The build representations that support answer very basic questions about sentence meaning, including "who" did "what" to "whom,"" etc. The AllenNLP SRL model is a reimplementation of a deep BiLSTM model (He et al, 2017). It closely matches state-of-the-art accuracy on benchmark datasets (including CoNLL 2005 shown below), while significantly improving training and and prediction runtimes.

Machine Comprehension Benchmarks

MC (machine comprehension) models answer natural language questions by selecting an answer span within an evidence text. The AllenNLP MC model is a PyTorch port of BiDAF, or Bi-Directional Attention Flow, a widely used MC baseline that achieves near state-of-the-art accuracies. Given a paragraph and a question, BiDAF will highlight the answer to the question in the paragraph provided. See how our approach compares with other systems on the SQuAD dataset.

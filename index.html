State-of-the-art models. AllenNLP supples high-quality deep learning models that balance quality and performance so they can easily be used for downstream tasks, whether you need to distribute them over a large dataset or run a few examples on your laptop without a GPU.

NLP framework for deep learning. AllenNLP provides a framework for easily training NLP models on PyTorch. Not only does AllenNLP provide a suite of real examples to get you started, but it also provides a high-level abstraction that handles details like padding and applying your models on example sentences.
